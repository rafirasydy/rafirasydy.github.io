Registering for Manchester

We're currently unable to register new users for PLR payments. However, the deadline to register for inclusion in next year's payments is not until 30 June 2024. We are making good progress towards having a registration system working in advance of this date.

Registering new titles and amending your details

Unfortunately, PLR online accounts are currently unavailable for users to amend personal details or register books. We're working hard to reinstate access before the end of the PLR year on 30 June 2024. In the meantime you can contact us and we'll advise you on the next steps to amend your details, or you can complete and send us this Additional Book Registration Form (PDF, 0.5mb) to register any new titles.

Please note, due to the current circumstances, you will be required to complete additional security checks including verification of your identity and postal address before we can make changes to your personal information. This is to help protect your account from potential fraudulent activity.

Legal deposit

Legal deposit is the statutory obligation to deposit at least one copy of every publication from the UK and Ireland, free of charge, at the British Library and other designated deposit libraries.

We are currently unable to receive digital legal deposit content via the publisher submission portal. Publishers who want to upload their titles via the portal are advised to contact the Digital Processing Team at the British Library by emailing LDO-Electronic@bl.uk, who will be happy to advise on alternative deposit options.

The Digital Processing Team will also forward requests from Scottish and Welsh publishers to their national deposit libraries, and requests from Irish publishers to the Library of Trinity College Dublin.

For those publishers who do not have an agreement to deposit digitally we are still accepting print legal deposit. To send us your publication, please post a copy to:
